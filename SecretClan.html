SecretClan

SecretClan was the sixth Clan created chronologically, after the Forest Clans and VibeClan.

The cats in SecretClan live in the area in front of Highstones, and eat mice and occaisionally rabbits. They were not named after a cat, but instead after their secrecy. Each cat has a second life due to being so close to the Moonstone, and, by extension, StarClan.

Their founder, Treestar, named them after their secrecy, and partly their first medicine cat's name.

General Personality

SecretClan cats are usually quiet, caring, and very independent. They are fiercely loyal to one another, but if a cat betrays them, then the cats can change from kind to hostile quicker than a wildfire spreads.

They are humorous at times.

They are helpful to the other Clans, and hate it when they are attacked by outside cats, such as rogues.
